n_jobs=None, refit=True, cv=4, verbose=1,

return_train_score=True) # cv is the best model.

1. scoring='roc_auc'
ngha: Ch nh metric (nh gi hiu sut) khi cross-validation.
roc_auc l ROC AUC score:

  • ROC = Receiver Operating Characteristic
  • AUC = Area Under the Curve
  • Gi tr t 0 1; cng gn 1, m hnh phn loi cng tt.

Dng roc_auc khi lm bi ton phn loi nh phn.
Nu khng ch nh, mc nh cross_validate dng score ca estimator (thng accuracy cho classifier).

2. cv=4
ngha: s lng folds trong k-fold cross-validation.
V d cv=4 ngha l:

  • Chia d liu thnh 4 phn (folds).
  • Lp 4 ln: mi ln chn 1 fold lm validation, 3 fold cn li lm training.
  • Tnh trung bnh metric trn 4 ln.

u im: nh gi model n nh hn, gim bias so vi train-test split n l.

3. return_train_score=True
ngha: cross_validate s tr v thm train score cho mi fold, ngoi test score.
