To ct "PriceperSqm" da vo bin "Price" v "Area"

Gi mi mt vung c tnh bng cng thc:

$\text{PriceperSqm} = \frac{\text{Price}}{\text{Area}}$.

Trong :

  • Price: gi bn ca cn nh
  • Area: din tch ca cn nh
  • PriceperSqm: gi trung bnh cho mi mt vung
